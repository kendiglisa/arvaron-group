**Step 3: Create a production build** Open your terminal in your project folder and run your build command (usually `npm run build`). This will generate a folder called `dist` (if you use Vite) or `build` (if you use Create React App). **Step 4: Drag & Drop Deploy** 1. Log in to [Netlify.com](https://app.netlify.com/). 2. Go to your **Sites** page. 3. Simply drag and drop that `dist` or `build` folder directly into the designated dropzone on the Netlify dashboard. 4. Your site is now live! **Where to see your submissions:** Once deployed, submit a test message through your live website. Then, go to your Netlify Dashboard, click on your site, and click the **"Forms"** tab on the left sidebar. You will see your active "contact" form and all incoming submissions there!
